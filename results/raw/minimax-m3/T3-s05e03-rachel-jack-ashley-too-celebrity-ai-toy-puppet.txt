# Feasibility Assessment: "AI Pop Star Dolls + Industry Medical Control" Thesis

**Date of assessment:** 2026-07-09
**Thesis under review:** A pop star's persona is packaged into AI dolls while the real person is medically controlled by industry.

**Overall assessment:** The thesis is a compound claim. Each half has different evidentiary status. Taken literally as a unified, systematic pattern, the claim **overshoots the evidence**. Each component, however, has documented sub-instances. I treat them separately below.

---

## Part A — "Persona is packaged into AI dolls"

**Evidence verdict:** Substantially supported for several specific artists; weaker as a universal pattern.

### Documented instances I'm confident about

| Case | What happened | Primary reporting I'm confident exists |
|---|---|---|
| "Heart on My Sleeve" (2023) | AI-generated song using cloned voices of Drake and The Weeknd; Universal Music Group had it pulled from streaming platforms. | UMG statement on AI-generated content; widespread coverage in NYT, BBC, Guardian (2023) |
| K/DA (2018–) | Virtual K-pop group using licensed likenesses/voices of real (Riot Games-affiliated) performers | Riot Games corporate site; Billboard coverage |
| aespa's "ae-aespa" virtual members (2020–) | SM Entertainment pairs real members with AI counterparts who appear in music videos, concerts | SM Entertainment announcements; reported in Variety, Soompi |
| Travis Scott / Ariana Grande virtual concerts | Real artists' likenesses used in Fortnite and other platforms | Epic Games announcements (2020, 2021); Variety coverage |
